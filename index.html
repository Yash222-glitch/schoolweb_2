Welcome to BOMIS

  • Birla Open Minds International School is located in the idyllic village of Selaqui in Uttarakhand.
  • With a large campus, Birla Open Minds International School is the best school in Dehradun. The CBSE-affiliated school provides an ideal setting for experiential and holistic education. The vision statement of the school emphasises a commitment to values, excellence, and leadership. It is one of the top international schools in Uttarakhand because it is at the forefront of comprehensive practises.
  • Every student has the opportunity to excel in his or her chosen field at school. All students are encouraged to set a series of goals for themselves, which are then prioritised. BOMIS has two distinct practises: goal setting and the Harkness table method.

Why BOMIS?

Birla Open Minds International School provides a nurturing environment wherein every child feels loved, cared, secured, respected & valued. The schools focus on 4 C’s: care, Co- operation, Collaboration and Courtesy. We firmly reiterate and restore what Tagore called the hallmark of childhood – ‘joy of learning’ and ‘the creative spirit’. BOMIS believes in the uniqueness of each child thereby fostering holistic development through integrated learning opportunities. We at BOMIS, Dehradun make sure that all the learning opportunities are child centric, teacher guided, integrates creativity, invention and innovation.

Vision

Birla Open Minds — a centre of academic excellence addresses the needs of the 21st century, by preparing individuals to become highly skilled, career oriented, economically independent and innovative, while also developing a sensitivity towards the economy, society and environment.

Mission

Birla Open Minds is a unique school community that works in collaboration with students and parents to provide quality education in a positive and supportive environment. The school achieves this goal by creating and implementing a world class curriculum that ensures that children are healthy, safe, disciplined, engaged, supported, challenged and prepared for the rigors of the current century.

Philosophy

The Birla Open Minds International Schools (BOMIS), firmly reiterate and restore what Tagore called the hallmark of childhood - 'joy of learning' and 'the creative spirit'. BOMIS believes in the uniqueness of each child thereby fostering holistic development through integrated learning opportunities.
